Sulit v1.0 Design System

Updated customer-facing presentation only.

Standardized:
- Primary, secondary, and destructive buttons
- Standard, recommendation, and summary cards
- One outline icon style
- Typography hierarchy
- 8/12/16/24/32 spacing scale
- Role-based colors
- Bottom sheets and selected states

Visible fixes:
- Search result name/size separation
- Calm Change Brand selection cards
- Removed My List floating menu
- Decision-first Best Prices Today screen
- Neutral store icons
- Proportionate Account layout
- White Profile rows and red-outline Log Out

Not changed:
Supabase, OCR, receipt logic, route calculations, prices, products, Admin business logic.
